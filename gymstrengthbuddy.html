Gym Strength Buddy

Gym Strength Buddy was an Android project we did for our school assignment. We wanted to make an app that was easy to use and stand out, since nowadays a lot of gym/exercise apps try to do everything and it might overwhelm the user.

Our app was focused on people who want to start or have already started strength training, the app itself is easy to use and the user only needs to add max weights for 4 different main exercises and the app will calculate the weights accordingly. The training and the calculations done for it follows Jim Wendlers 5/3/1 program.

We used Android studio and Java as the coding language, since our course was taught in Java. I myself worked on the code and on the design side using Android Studio.

Download the APK to test the app on your Android phone
