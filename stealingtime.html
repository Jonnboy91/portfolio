Stealing Time

We just started our game development course in mid-March and already in start of May, me and few of my classmates decided to participate to a Game Jam. we had one month to do everything from scratch. I myself worked on the scripts and the animations for the game, I also worked on the levels and gameplay after the main design (how everything looked) had been done.

The theme for the Game Jam was time and we wanted to play around with the concept of time both in gameplay and in story. Overall we were really happy about what we made and from +200 entries we also got good ranking in different areas.

Check out the Itch.io submission site for comments that we got and also to download the game and the code with it
